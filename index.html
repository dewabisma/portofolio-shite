I have been teaching for almost 2 years now. I have taught varied range of age groups, from kindergarten to adult. I mainly teach English but sometimes I do teach other subject like Math for elementary student.

Teaching has brought a color to my life. When I can help my student to do what they think they can't do, it really makes me feel proud. My Developer Life

The first time I get to taste programming was when I was in senior highschool. That time I was taught the pascal programming language by my teacher. Later, I decided to learn android development. It was fun but I couldn't put myself totally into it so stopped halfway.

After a while, web development caught my interest. I didn't want to make the same half-hearted decision. So, I decided to learn web development seriously. My main sources for learning are youtube and udemy. Traversy Media is one of the reason I can be what I'm today. The channel has so many great content that is shared for free. I have endless gratitude for Brad for creating that youtube channel. Beside youtube, I also learn from the courses in Udemy. It's a great place for finding online course with affordable price.

Beside online courses, I have also participate in a short coding bootcamp that is held by BDI Denpasar. In the bootcamp, I learned some best practice of starting a project. The best thing for me is the new friends that I made there!
